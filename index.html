How to make a cake?

  • Cut 2 apples into slices (with or without skin).
  • Fry apple slices on a pan with oil or butter for short time (around 5 minutes)on medium heat, add sugar (white or brown), optional choice is to add a bit of lemon juice until everything has mixed and sugar melted, put them on plate to cool down.
  • Break 2 eggs in a bowl, add 130g sugar, 60g oil, 60g yoghurt or cottage cheese (might need a little vit of milk), mix everything.
  • Pour 200g of flour through sieve or add straight to the bowl.
  • Add 8g of baking soda. Mix until smooth.
  • Pour into cake form, add apple slices on top.
  • Bake at 180Â° C for 40-50 minutes
